# Pricing

First commercial pricing can be per active student per month.

## Simple Model

- Starter: 1 to 20 students, fixed monthly minimum
- Studio: 21 to 100 students, per-student pricing
- School: 100+ students, volume pricing

## Suggested Numbers

For early China-market or studio-market testing:

- RMB 3 to 5 per active student per month
- Minimum monthly fee for very small studios
- Optional setup fee for camera integration

Example:

- 10 students at RMB 5 each: RMB 50/month
- 100 students at RMB 5 each: RMB 500/month
- Camera integration: one-time setup fee or premium tier

## Add-ons

- Monitoring camera integration
- Multi-room support
- Parent attendance notifications
- Advanced reports
- Cloud backup
- White-label deployment
- Robot skill integration

## Why Per Student Works

The value scales with the number of students:

- More students means more attendance time saved.
- More students means more reporting and parent communication value.
- The buyer can understand the bill immediately.
